Game Designer/Artist

If you are a creative person who is responsible for the design and layout of a video game, create the storylines, characters, levels, scenarios, etc. of a certain game and have past experiences.

Game developers

Video game developers, also known as game developers, are responsible for designing and developing video games for PC, console, and mobile applications. Their job is to code the base engine from the ideas of the design team. They may also be involved in character design, level design, animation, and unit testing.

Blockchain Engineers

Blockchain engineers specialize in creating and implementing digital solutions for organizations by utilizing a unique type of technology. Blockchain technology allows information to be distributed and shared publicly via the Internet without being copied. The information is not stored in a central location.

Marketing Team

Marketers are responsible for planning, creating, and executing marketing campaigns to expand their company's reach and potential customer pipeline.

Economist

Prepare reports, tables, and charts. Economists study the production and distribution of resources, goods, and services by collecting and analyzing data, researching trends, and evaluating economic issues. Must have basic understanding of cryptology.
