MGHS Services Business Profile

Established in 2011, MGHS SERVICES is a one-stop shop for start-ups and SMEs. We offer a variety of solutions to support, improve, and grow your business at the stage of conception, launch of a product or service, and expansion of your home business model into an official business.

We provide innovative solutions for starting a business in the Philippine market. It also recovers from adversity, leverages technology, stimulates growth, adheres to government procedures, enters the market through marketing packages, content establishment (Web), social media support, integrates training and workshops, and starts a business. Empower your home and complement small businesses and start-ups.

MGHS Services helps clients to conceptualize and plan their business. We prepare feasibility studies, marketing plans, facilitate registration and licensing to relevant government agencies, and provide management consulting and coaching.
