MGHS Services Internship Program

Start your Internship Here in MGHS

Project-based learning is an approach to education that allows interns to apply their classroom experiences to projects that address "real-world problems. The company produces graduates who are technically competent and possess other skills like the capability to learn through experience, collaboration, critical thinking, practical aptitude, and the ability to synthesize the solution. It recognizes that not all aspects of learning can be taught in the classroom.

Goals and Objectives

The internship aims to satisfy curiosity and hone research potential. To enable students to understand the functional behavior of man-machine management, entrepreneurship, industrial safety, and to help students develop their personality and soft skills. To expose students to potential employers; and to provide opportunities to work with industrial practitioners.

Execution of Internship

The period of internship would be according to the designated hours mentioned by the school. The internship will be held virtually and interns should ensure that they will be working from home under CHED and respective school/university guidelines.

Here are the requirements to pass if you want to take your internship with us.

  • Updated Resume and Curriculum Vitae.
  • Original letter of Endorsement from School/University.
  • 2pcs. 1x1 ID picture.
  • Scanned copy of School ID Card.
  • Original copy of Barangay Clearance.
  • Course Curriculum/Prospectus
  • Other School requirements for internship

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

     
  • How soon may I be informed about the outcome of my application?

    It varies from case to case. In the majority of cases, selected applicants are notified after 5-7 days.

  • When is the starting date of my internship?

    We can start your internship as soon as you are ready and comply with our requirements. To credit all the hours rendered, we will start your internship based on your academic calendar.

  • Will I get paid this Internship?

    No, this is a free internship. We believe that not all benefits can get through money. That's why we are offering valuable MELA access to our interns.

  • Can I submit my Barangay clearance later?

    Yes, you can submit it later.

  • Do you have website or social media accounts?

    Our Business was long ago established with a physical office location but due to the pandemic, we've decided to transfer to online services. All our social media presence is now in the building process.

  • Would you provide equipment or an internet connection?

    No, our first requirement is for you to have the equipment and a stable internet connection and we are expecting you to be ready to join immediately.

  • Any other internship work you offer aside from the project you have?

    You may suggest what is a good enough project that may be related to your skill and area of field. This is subject to review.

  • Can I take the internship ahead of my internship schedule?

    No, internship schedules should be in accordance to your university calendar.
